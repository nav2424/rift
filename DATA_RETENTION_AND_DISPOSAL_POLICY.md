## 1. Purpose

This policy defines how Rift retains, manages, and disposes of consumer data in accordance with data minimization principles, applicable privacy laws, and operational requirements.

---

## 2. Scope of Data Covered

This policy applies to consumer data processed through the Rift platform, including:

- User profile information (name, email, phone number)
- Transaction and platform activity data
- Messages and files exchanged in transactions
- Verification and compliance-related metadata (when applicable)
- Payment-related metadata (excluding payment credentials)

**Rift does not store:**
- Credit card numbers
- Bank account numbers
- Payment credentials

All payment processing is handled directly by Stripe.

---

## 3. Data Retention Principles

Rift retains consumer data only for as long as necessary to:

- Operate and maintain the platform
- Facilitate transactions and payouts
- Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
- Meet legal, accounting, and regulatory obligations
- Support fraud prevention and platform security

Retention is purpose-based, not indefinite.

---

## 4. Retention Duration

- Consumer data is retained while an account remains active.
- Data required for legal, financial, or compliance purposes is retained for the minimum period required by applicable law.
- Data that is no longer required for operational or legal purposes is scheduled for deletion or anonymization.
- No consumer data is retained longer than necessary.

---

## 5. Data Deletion and Disposal

### Account Deletion

- Users may request account deletion through the platform.
- Prior to deletion, users must complete or cancel all active transactions and withdraw available funds.
- Upon deletion, user data is permanently removed through cascading deletion processes.

### Disposal Methods

- Data is deleted from primary databases.
- Deletion applies to backups where technically feasible.
- Residual data retained for legal obligations is access-restricted and deleted once no longer required.

---

## 6. User Rights

Depending on jurisdiction, users may have the right to:

- Access their data
- Update or correct their information
- Request deletion of their data

Deletion requests are honored unless retention is legally required.

---

## 7. Data Security During Retention

- All stored data is encrypted at rest using vendor-managed infrastructure.
- Data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher.
- Access to retained data is restricted via role-based access controls.
- No credentials or sensitive authentication data are logged.

---

## 8. Policy Review and Updates

This policy is reviewed periodically and updated as:

- Platform functionality evolves
- Regulatory requirements change
- Additional security or privacy controls are implemented

---

## 9. Responsibility

Responsibility for enforcement of this policy lies with Rift's administrative and security leadership.
